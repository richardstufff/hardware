Since Esau and Joyce Ghany founded the company in 1976, our operations have grown substantially - based firmly on our commitment to always go above and beyond, offer the very best products, and deliver unparalleled levels of service to our customers. Our privately-owned company is run by the founder's children, Peter and Patricia Ghany, and is based in La Romaine, Trinidad - home to our Head Office and central warehousing facility.With nearly 40 staff, we now have an established presence in Trinidad and Tobago, Suriname, Colombia, Argentina, USA, and Peru. We are continuing to expand our business into new markets, with our latest joint venture launching in Guyana.

ESAU has been involved in every major oil and gas project in T&T for the past forty years. Our capacity to successfully take on large-scale projects sets us apart from other suppliers and we have received several awards including Velan's Top Distributor since 2005 and BP's Five Star Supplier for the Mango and Cashima Gas Platforms.

We are the market leader in our field and are committed to supplying all of our customers' product needs. We stock a wide range of specialty products and keep up with international trends to supply the latest innovations to the market. Our industry and product experts work with customers to identify their requirements and manage their order from the point of purchase to delivery. We offer 24/7 customer service and, in Trinidad, deliver daily using our in-house fleet of drivers to supply customers nationwide. We have invested in a comprehensive order and inventory management system to ensure we are able to efficiently stock and deliver products.

We are committed to recruiting high quality, diverse staff and fostering a great working dynamic amongst the tight knit team. Our employees are our greatest asset - we are dedicated to prioritizing their safety and developing talent within the company. In keeping with our company values, we have a reputation for our long-standing relationships and personalized approach with staff, customers, and the manufacturers that we represent.

ESAU upholds socially responsible practices through our Directors' active involvement in various initiatives and organizations. We strive to be as green as possible and encourage a culture of recycling and responsible disposal practices. With a reputation for our strong ethos around customer service, teamwork, business ethics, community, and excellence, we solve problems using solid, value-driven solutions.

Mission

To be the preferred "Supplier of Choice" in markets we serve by building strong relationships with our customers and suppliers.


Vision

To be the leading distribution Company in Caribbean of pipe, valves and pipe fitting products to the energy and industrial sectors.
